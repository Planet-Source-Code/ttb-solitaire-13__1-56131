in this game you want to make pairs of cards that equal 13 until all
cards in the pile and in the pyramid are gone.you make pairs by 
dragging open cards onto other open cards, for example, 10 onto a 3,
2 on a jack, ace on a queen. kings are 13 soyou just need to double
click them. you double click the pile to unveil 3 more open cards.
open cards are ones that have no cards covering part of them.
